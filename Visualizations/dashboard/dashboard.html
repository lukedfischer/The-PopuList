Symbol Explanation

This dashboard summarizes whether a party of country on the PopuList is populist, far-right, far-left, eurosceptic, and in parliament. The following symbols are used. Please refer to the main website for additional information.

  • A ● indicates that a party fulfills a characteristic
  • A ◐ indicates a borderline case
  • At times, the specific time intervals for when the characteristic was fulfilled is provided in parentheses
